Deadly Crackdown on November 2019 Protests in Iran

November 15
Iran imposed rationing and raised the price of fuel. It announced the change overnight, which caught many citizens by surprise. Hundreds of people filled the streets in multiple cities including Mashhad, Ahvaz, Behbahan, Mahshahr, and Omidiyeh to protest the overnight increase in fuel prices.
November 16
Protests reportedly devolved into violence in several cities. Police forces used tear gas and water cannons to disperse protesters.
